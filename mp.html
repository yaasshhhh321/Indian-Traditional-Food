BHUTTE KA KESS

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups of grated sweet corn
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Curry leaves 5-7
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tsp chopped green chillies
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Coriander to Garnish

    • INSTRUCTIONS

  • In a non-stick pan, put in ghee and add the mustard, cumin and curry leaves.
  • Once it crackles, add ginger and chillies, stir it for a minute.
  • Now add the grated corn, salt and turmeric.
  • Mix it well. Cover with a lid for about 20 mins but keep stirring at regular intervals.
  • Once the corn starts leaving the sides of the pan, add lime juice and sugar.
  • Stir again.
  • Garnish with coriander and serve it hot.
