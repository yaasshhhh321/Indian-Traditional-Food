DHUSKA

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1/4 cup Black gram dal
  • 1 Tbsp Bengal gram dal
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • To taste Salt
  • For deep frying Oil

    • INSTRUCTIONS

  • Soak the dal and rice together for 6 hrs or overnight.
  • Grind it into a smooth paste with the required amount of water. The batter should not be very runny
  • Add salt and cumin seeds to it and mix well
  • Let it fry in medium flame till it turns golden brown.
  • Now flip it over and cook on the other side. Drain it on an absorbent paper
  • Serve hot with Ghugni
