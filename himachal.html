BABRU

INGREDIENTS

  • Wheat flour - 300g
  • Baking powder - 0.5 tsp
  • Ghee/Clarified butter - 2tbsp
  • Brown sugar or Jaggery powder - 75g
  • Water - 75ml
  • Fennel Seeds/ Saunf (optional) - Approx 10 g.
  • Sunflower oil - for deep frying.
  • You can use as much oil as necessary for deep frying.

    • INSTRUCTIONS

  • Soak the daal in warm water and leave overnight.
  • Clean and husk the dal and grind it to a paste.
  • Add the baking soda to the flour along with some water and knead it well.
  • Make small rounds from the dough and add the prepared dal to each ball.
  • Roll it like a chapati and deep-fry it. Serve hot with any sabzi.
