DALMA

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups cooked Toor Dal (Pigeon Pea Lentils)
  • 1 Drumstick, peeled and chopped
  • 1 Raw Banana, peeled and diced
  • 1 big Potato, peeled and diced
  • 10-12 Beans, diced
  • 0.5 cup diced Pumpkin
  • 2 Brinjals, diced
  • 1 Tomato, roughly chopped
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 2 Tbsp Mustard Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Panch Phoran (whole spice mix)
  • 1 Bayleaf
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 cups Water
  • Salt to taste

    • INSTRUCTIONS

  • Add cooked toor dal (lentils) to a deep pan or kadhai. Add water as required and 1 tsp turmeric powder. Mix well.
  • Add in the drumsticks, raw banana, pumpkin, potato and beans.
  • Add salt to taste. Cover and cook until the vegetables are half cooked.
  • Add in the brinjal and cover and cook until the vegetables are almost cooked.
  • Add in the chopped tomato and cook until all the vegetables are cooked.
  • Adjust seasoning if required. Remove from heat and keep aside.
  • To make the tempering, heat mustard oil in a pan and add the panch phoran spice mix.
  • Once the spices splutter, add in the bay leaf and sliced onions.
  • Fry until the onions are translucent.
  • Add in red chilli powder and the cumin powder and saute for 1 minute.
  • Add the Dalma to the tempering and simmer for 5-6 minutes.
  • Add water if required.
  • Garnish with the chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with rice or roti.
