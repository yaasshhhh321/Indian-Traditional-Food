KAFULI

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 bunch spinach
  • 1 bunch fenugreek leaves
  • 3 green chilly
  • 2 tablespoon oil (preferably mustard oil)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • a pinch of asafoetida
  • 2 inch ginger
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilly powder
  • 1 tablespoon curd
  • 1 teaspoon rice flour
  • salt to taste

    • INSTRUCTIONS

  • Wash the green leaves and chop them.
  • Boil spinach, fenugreek leaves and green chillies with half a cup of water.
  • Make a paste of ginger and garlic.
  • Make a puree of boiled green leaves.
  • Take oil in a pan, add cumin. As it splutters, put ginger-garlic paste to it and saute for sometime.
  • Add the puree, turmeric powder, coriander powder, salt and water according to the consistency required.
  • Let cook for sometime.
  • Make a paste of rice flour in a little water and put it.
  • Blend the curds and add it.
  • Let simmer for sometime.
  • Kafuli is ready, serve it phulka or chapati.
