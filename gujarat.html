BAJRI NA ROTLO

INGREDIENTS

  • Bajra (black millet) flour
  • Salt
  • Bajra (black millet) flour
  • Melted Ghee

    • INSTRUCTIONS

  • In a bowl, add bajra flour, salt and enough lukewarm water and knead into a semi- soft dough and keep kneading for 3 to 4 minutes or till the dough is very smooth.
  • Divide the dough into equal portions and roll a portion of the dough into a 125 mm. (5") diameter circle and dust a little bajra flour.
  • Heat a non-stick tawa (griddle) on a high flame and when the tawa is hot, place the rotla gently over it.
  • Cook it till small blisters appear on the surface and flip it on the other side and cook for a few more seconds.
  • Cook it on an open flame till it puffs up and brown spots appear on both the sides and serve it with any chutney of your choice
