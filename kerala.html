PUTTU AND KADALA CURRY

INGREDIENTS

  • Puttu - Ingredients

  • Coarse Rice Flour - 1 cup
  • Grated Coconut - 1/2 cup
  • Water - As Required
  • Salt - To Taste

  • Kadala Curry - Ingredients

  • Black Chickpea - 1 kg
  • Coconut Oil - 100 ml
  • Grated Coconut - 3
  • Garlic - 10 g
  • Shallots - 150 g
  • Mustard Seeds - 10 g
  • Red Chilli - 50 g
  • Sliced Onion - 500 g
  • Whole Coriander - 50 g
  • Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp.
  • Red Chilli Powder - 1/2 tsp.
  • Curry Leaves - 2 sprigs
  • Garam Masala Powder - 2 tsp.
  • Salt - To Taste

    • INSTRUCTIONS

  • Boil black chickpea in salt. Sauté grated coconut, shallots, red chillies, coriander, curry leaves and garlic in a little coconut oil till golden brown.
  • After that grind them together to a coarse paste.
  • Sauté the mustard seeds, sliced onions, coriander powder, chilli powder, and garam masala powder and fry well.
  • Add water and the boiled kadala.
  • Cook well. Add a little salt and serve hot.
